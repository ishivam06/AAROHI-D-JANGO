Campus Ambassador is a programme which allows you to collaborate closely with top management of leading events in the region while simultaneously encouraging you to develop your communication, and managerial talents. Campus Ambassadors are representatives of the event to their institutes and are crucial in giving many events the reach they need

As a Campus Ambassador you are expected to help Aarohi'22 exngage on multiple platforms at multipe strata within your campus. You are in-charge of Aarohi'22 for your campus. You will get the opportunity to host workshops and events firsthand, hold PORs on behalf of Aarohi'22 and handle all publicity affairs for your campus

Campus Ambassador also presents an excellent opportunity as leading Campus Ambassadors get internship opportunities with various partners, free entry to all Aarohi'22 events along with free merch and much much more

Q: Who is a Campus Ambassador?
He/She is the first point of contact and the publicity head of Aarohi in his/her respective college.
Q: What kind of responsibilities a CA has to fulfill?
CA has to share the posts of Aarohi through various social media platforms and posters of various events of Aarohi in his/her college notice board.
Q: Would we have to work outside the college campus or prepare promotional materials?
No. Your work is to help us extend our reach only on your college campus. Also, promotional materials will be prepared by us and shared with you through WhatsApp or Facebook groups that you will be a part of.
Q: What to do if i have any other queries?
You can drop a mail to ca@aarohiworld.org
